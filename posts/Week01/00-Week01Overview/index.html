Welcome to Week 1! This week, we'll dive right into working with Swift to build the foundations of an app that we'll work on all semester long. If you haven't gone through the material from "Week 0", please do so before proceeding; that will ensure your development environment is setup correctly, which is important when following along with the material in this course as well as completing and handing in assignments. Here's an overview of what we'll cover this week:

  • Introduction to Swift
    • We'll go over the basics of Swift, show how it is similar to other languages like Java, and most importantly highlight what makes Swift different from the other languages out there - and how programmers can take advantage of those differences when developing their apps.
  • Programming Assignment 1
    • Your first assignment will center around making a model for a ToDo app, to get us ready to work on the user interface next week.
  • Development Log 1
    • This week you'll also work on your first Development Log - where you'll define the requirements for the app you'll design and make as your final project for the class.
