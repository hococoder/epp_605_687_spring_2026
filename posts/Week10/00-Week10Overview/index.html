Welcome to Week 10! This week we'll take a look at the basics of App Intents. App Intents have been around for a few years in different forms, but this year they have been reimagined to support Apple Intelligence. This week and next you'll learn about APp Intents, App Entities, and the various places you can use App Intents in your app. This week though, you'll learn about:

  • Introduction to App Intents - what are App Intents, and why should you use them in your app?
  • The AppEntity Protocol - model types in your app can be represented as App Entities, which can be used with App Intents - think of these as nouns.
  • The AppIntent Protocol - while App Entities represent model objects, App Intents represent things you can do with those entities - these are the verbs.
  • Getting Ready for App Intents - what do you need to do to prep your app for App Intents?
  • Using App Intents with Search - Search is just one system framework that easily integrates into the App Intents framework. Learn how you can share your entities with the system to make search more powerful.
  • Programming Assignment 10 - in this assignment, you'll apply these technologies to a Meal Planning app.
