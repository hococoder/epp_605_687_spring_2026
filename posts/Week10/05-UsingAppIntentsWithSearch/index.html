Overview

Now since you have your AppEntity and AppIntent structs defined, and hooked into the OS, let's try giving it a run and see what happens

Running the App

The video above does the following:

  • Preps the settings of the simulator to allow for donations and display of entities on the search screen.
  • Runs the app.
  • Waits a little bit to let the entities get donated (this can be particularly annoying on the simulator).
  • Hit the home button to put the app in the background.
  • Drag down on the home screen to show search.
  • Start typing the name of a lesson.
  • The entity in question should display in the results - tap it!

You'll notice when the lesson was tapped, nothing happened! In week 11, you'll dive more into intents to do more with your entities once they are selected.
