Week 6 Overview

Welcome to Week 6! This week we'll dive into some other frameworks in iOS that you can use to add functionality, a great experience, and even a little fun to your app.

  • Introduction to Core Image: Core Image is the framework that handles functionality such as face detection and applying filters and other operations to images
  • Vision Framework: iOS has some great built in capabilities related to detecting objects in pictures, including faces.
  • Making Custom Controls: Sometimes the built in UI widgets just don't meet your designer's needs - that's where the ability to make custom controls comes into play, both with CoreGraphics and SwiftUI.
  • SwiftUI Charts: at some point you'll probably want to display a chart in your app - SwiftUI Charts now provides a built in set of functionality to help you with that.
  • Programming Assignment 6: When is the coffee shop busy? You'll add SwiftUI charts to show the user that info.
